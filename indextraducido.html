Description

Soccer products from different teams and brands in general are sold, as well as accessories used for the practice of this sport (shin guards, whistles, cones, wrist guards, socks, etc.)

Objetivo

Publish sports products of sports teams, franchises or brands through a website, for football fans, developed in the period of the semester September-December 2021.

What is CUU SPORTS?

This is the place where the biggest soccer fans meet.

In it you will find all the products and accessories that are necessary to practice the sport that moves crowds, soccer.

History

In the first weeks of September 2021, a group of 4 initial members was formed, which were: Arturo Ceceña, Juan Castro, Edgar Hernández and Carlos Gallegos, to carry out a website as an integrating project. At the beginning, there was no clear idea, so a brainstorm was carried out, as well as a vote to define the theme of the site. In the end we all agreed on the same idea, which was soccer. Shortly afterwards it was agreed that the website would consist of a series of catalogs referring to soccer products. A few weeks after starting the project, Arturo Ceceña dropped out of the university, so there were only 3 members left on the team. It was thus that the website was carried out, implementing the knowledge that we were learning in the subjects that we took. Finally after many modifications the site was ready on the day: 12/2/2021 going into operation that same day.

Mission

Vision
